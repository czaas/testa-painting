About Testa Painting

Testa painting is a full service painting company which offers exceptional customer service and attention to detail.

We only use quality paints and trained courteous painters. Testa Painting serves San Luis and Santa Barbara Counties with most of our work in the Five Cities and Santa Maria area. We paint interior and exterior of residences. Refinish cabinets and decks. Also have pressure washing and gutter cleaning services.
